Abstract

[cite_start]This paper proposes that Nature operates as a "causality killing machine" within an overall Null-Universe[cite: 3, 4]. [cite_start]It presents a no-boundary, no-unique-beginning proposal for the universal phenomenon[cite: 12]. Core Arguments

  • [cite_start]Black Holes: Described as "saints among us mortals"[cite: 83]. [cite_start]They always expand and evaporate external space-time via Hawking radiation, which effectively kills causality in the surrounding space-time[cite: 17].
